James McNulty

Senior Marketing Strategist & Songwriter

Blog

James McNulty, 35, was born in Sidcup, Kent (England). James grew up for a short while in Wamberal, New South Wales, Australia. James currently lives in Texas, USA with his wife Megan, daughter Penelope, and two dogs Colin and Davey.

James has been developing websites since the early 2000's, and has been passionate about search engine optimization began around 2012. James currently works full-time as a Senior Marketing Strategist at UpBuild, an all-remote boutique digital marketing team specializing in the end-to-end optimization of the online user journey.

James is also an avid music fan, has played guitar in a couple of bands, and enjoys writing and recording at home.
