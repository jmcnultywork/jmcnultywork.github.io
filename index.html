About James

James McNulty, 34, was born and raised in England and Australia. James currently lives in Texas, USA with his wife Megan and two dogs Colin and Davey.

James began developing websites in the early 2000's, and transitioned into search engine optimization several years ago. James currently works full-time at UpBuild, an all-remote boutique digital marketing agency specializing in the end-to-end optimization of the online user journey.

James is an avid pop-punk fan, has played in a couple of bands, and enjoys writing and recording music at home.
