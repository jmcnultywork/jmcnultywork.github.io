About James

James McNulty, 34, was born and raised in England and Australia. James currently lives in Texas, USA with his wife Megan and two dogs Colin and Davey.

James works full-time at UpBuild, an all-remote boutique digital marketing agency specializing in the end-to-end optimization of the online user journey. Since the early 2000's, James grew up learning how to build websites and transitioned from a web development role to SEO several years ago. He is an avid pop-punk fan, has played in two bands (Sonnet and Rival Antics), and still enjoys writing and recording music.
